The Xazna AI platform offers a range of models for chatbot, text to speech (TTS) and speech to text (STT) functionalities. Below, you can explore the available models and their intended uses.

## Chatbot

- **Base**: This chatbot gives clear and natural responses, expresses emotions, and makes conversations more engaging and interactive.

## Text to speech (TTS)

- **SurAio**: This model has improved voice clarity, expressiveness, and support for emotional voice tones. It is perfect for applications needing dynamic and engaging audio output.

## Speech to text (STT)

- **Base**: This model is designed to convert speech into text with reliable accuracy. Further details on its performance and supported languages will be available as it matures.
